Personal Interests

Hi, I'm Marshall, but my friends also call me "Micro" (hence "Micro Micro")!

I'm a Vietnamese-American artist currently studying art at New College of Florida.

In my freetime I often draw and paint, but I also enjoy playing video games. I'll often make art related to the games I play, including Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild and 原神.

My favorite games are Breath of the Wild, Hades by Supergiant Games, Undertale, Animal Crossing: New Horizons, and recently 太鼓の達人: Drum ‘n’ Fun!

Apart from art and video games, I also enjoy learning about linguistics, floriography, and human behavior.
